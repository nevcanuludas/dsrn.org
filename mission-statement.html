Mission Statement The Diving Standards & Recognition Network (DSRN) is dedicated to promoting clarity, transparency, and safety in diving education worldwide. By providing a neutral platform to compare training organizations, map certification equivalencies, and highlight quality and safety standards, DSRN enables divers, instructors, and agencies to make informed decisions. Through collaboration with diving organizations and stakeholders, DSRN strives to establish a trusted, globally recognized reference for technical, recreational, and scientific diving standards.
