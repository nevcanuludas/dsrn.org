Diving Standards & Recognition Network

The Diving Standards & Recognition Network (DSRN) is a global initiative dedicated to improving safety, transparency, and consistency across the diving industry.

We connect standards, organizations, and professionals under a unified framework—helping divers, instructors, and institutions navigate certifications with clarity and trust.
